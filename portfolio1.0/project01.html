huawei g9 plus: that's enough

That's enough is a campaign for HUAWEI G9 Plus launch. On the warm-up stage, we used ridiculous exaggeration to present the customers' high expectation on certain products, suggesting "that's enough for all your demands. We understand and they shall be met"; after the launch of HUAWEI G9 Plus, through a series of metaphorical depiction of product features combination, we presented that our product provided enough functions to suit your need.
